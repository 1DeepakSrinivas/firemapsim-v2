## Weather inputs

### Manual overrides

Editable values:

- wind speed,
- wind direction,
- temperature,
- humidity.

### Weather lookup

Lookup supports:

- city,
- street address,
- US ZIP.

On success, current conditions are loaded and applied to scenario state.

## Terrain overlays

Available layers:

- fuel,
- slope,
- aspect.

You can:

- load layer data,
- switch active layer,
- toggle per-cell info cursor.

## Troubleshooting pointers

- weather lookup errors: verify query format and US scope,
- terrain load errors: retry after confirming project location and run context.
