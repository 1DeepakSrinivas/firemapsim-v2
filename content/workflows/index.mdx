FireMapSim-v2 supports two setup patterns plus a replay-focused analysis flow.

## Available workflow paths

- [Manual Setup](/docs/workflows/manual-setup)
- [Guided Chat Setup](/docs/workflows/guided-chat-setup)
- [Replay Analysis](/docs/workflows/replay-analysis)

## Choosing the right workflow

Use manual setup when:

- you already know your scenario inputs,
- you need precise low-level control quickly.

Use guided chat when:

- you want step-by-step prompts,
- you are iterating on scenario intent and defaults.

Use replay analysis when:

- you need to explain spread behavior,
- you are validating the impact of changed inputs.
